    About Us

    Working for Women

    We at The Habibti believe that every woman at work should have access to safe, non-toxic, high quality Period Products at all the times. We are bringing best-in-class solutions for corporates and organizations to enable their women employees have stress-free periods while at work.

    Enable

    In the UAE more than 40% women are spending about 8-10 hours everyday working at their workplaces. With this trend growing upwards, The Habibti wants to ensure hygiene products for women at workplaces be treated like a basic necessity.

    Empower

    Women face several challenges when dealing with period problems, and unavailability to period products at point of need is one of the major one.The Habibti comes as a ready solution with emphasis on making periods stress-free at workplaces.

    Celebrate

    Communities at large are celebrating initiatives supporting and encouraging women employees. Acknowledging and respecting period problems is also a step in that direction. Organizations that care for their women employees must adopt & propagate The Habibti.

    Our Technology

    The Habibti works through a Smart IoT-enabled Dispensing Mechanism which is interactive and 100% germ-free. The user makes product choice on the touchscreen and flashes TAG for easy dispensing. Our TAG is a small 25mm sticker which can be stuck on mobile covers, ID cards, wallets, etc.
